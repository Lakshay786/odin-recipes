Garlic Bread

Welcome to my page! Today we will learn about how to make Garlic Bread!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) of active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490 g) bread flour
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps:

  1. Sprinkle yeast over warm water in a small bowl, gently stir, and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In the bowl of a standing electric mixer, add the flour, olive oil, salt, and sugar. Use a paddle attachment and mix on low speed for a minute.
  3. Slowly pour the warm yeast water into the bowl with the mixer running. Increase the speed to medium and mix until the dough is smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.
  4. Form the dough into a round and coat it lightly with olive oil. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rise until it doubles in size, 1-2 hours.
  5. Divide the dough into two balls and let them rest for 10 minutes. Working one piece at a time, on a lightly floured surface, flatten the dough into a disk and then pull and stretch the edges to form a 12-inch circle.
  6. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or pizza stone. Add your toppings and bake in a 500°F oven for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Voila! Your Garlic Bread is ready.
